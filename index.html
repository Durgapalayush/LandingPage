    ON MOON

There were six crewed landings between 1969 and 1972,
and numerous uncrewed landings.
All crewed missions to the Moon were conducted by the Apollo program, with the last departing the
lunar surface in December 1972. After Luna 24 in 1976 there were no soft landings on the Moon until Chang'e 3 in 2013.
